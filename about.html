Covenant Standers is a Philadelphia area women's weekly bible study and encouragement meeting for wives in a troubled covenant marriage or separated / divorced from their covenant spouse. Our focus is to stand faithful in Christ for marriage restoration in the midst of strife, separation, or divorce from our covenant spouse.

Women who have been called by God to pray for the restoration of their marriages will find support for their stand here. We rely on Christ's healing power for ourselves, for our family, and for our marriage.

We will use podcasts and books from Rejoice Marriage Ministries as a teaching tool. We encourage you to sign up for the Charlene Cares Daily Devotional from their ministry for daily support in your stand.
