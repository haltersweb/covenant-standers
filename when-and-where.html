When & Where?
Standers Meetings

"Again, truly I tell you that if two of you on earth agree about anything they ask for, it will be done for them by my Father in heaven. For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." — Matthew 18:19-20

Covenant Standers women's ministry meets weekly at Narberth Presbyterian Church, just outside the Philadelphia Area, about 10 miles northwest of Center City Philadelphia.

The church is in walking distance from the Narberth Train Station on the Paoli-Thorndale Regional Rail Line.

Thursday evenings, 7:00 – 8:15
